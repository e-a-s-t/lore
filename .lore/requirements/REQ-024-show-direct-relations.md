# REQ-024 - Show Direct Relations

## Requirement

Lore SHALL support displaying direct relationships between artifacts.

## Rationale

Understanding surrounding context is often as important as understanding the artifact itself.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Outgoing relations can be displayed.
- [ ] Incoming relations can be displayed.
- [ ] Relation types are preserved.
- [ ] Missing references are clearly indicated.
