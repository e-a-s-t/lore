# REQ - Self Hosting

## Requirement

Lore SHALL have zero mandatory external services.

## Rationale

Lore should be usable in private repositories, customer environments and offline systems.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Everything required for normal operation runs locally.
- [ ] No SaaS service is mandatory.
